Our Mission

Real change happens when support goes beyond survival.

Jeevora Foundation focuses on long-term transformation — supporting children and young adults through nutrition, education, mentorship, skill-building, and career readiness.

We believe that every young person deserves not just a meal today, but a meaningful path forward. Our work bridges the gap between urgent need and lasting opportunity — one child, one community, one future at a time.

The Reality We Face

The problems that still remain unseen.

Millions of children across India grow up without the support they need. This is the reality Jeevora Foundation was created to change.

School Dropouts

Economic pressure forces millions of children to leave school before completing a basic education — cutting their future short before it begins.

1 in 3 children leave school early

Child Hunger

Hunger impacts focus, attendance, and long-term health. For many children, a school meal is the only reliable nutrition they receive each day.

190M+ children face undernourishment

Lack of Skills

Without access to skill training, young adults are unable to compete for jobs — remaining trapped in cycles of poverty despite their ambition and potential.

47% youth lack vocational access

Youth Unemployment

Millions of young Indians are ready to work but lack guidance, mentorship, and connections — leaving talent unused and potential unfulfilled.

23% youth unemployment rate
The Transformation

From a single meal to a career built with pride.

Every life we touch follows a path of four transformative steps — a journey from need to possibility.

1
Step One

Support — The Foundation of Everything

Before a child can dream, they need to feel safe, seen, and nourished. We begin every relationship with warmth — providing regular meals, school supplies, and the gentle reassurance that they matter.

  • Daily nutritious meal programmes
  • School material and uniform support
  • Emotional and wellbeing check-ins
2
Step Two

Education — Opening the First Door

With support in place, learning begins. We work alongside schools and community educators to ensure children don't just attend — they engage, explore, and grow in confidence every day.

  • Remedial coaching and tutoring
  • Literacy and numeracy programmes
  • Creative and experiential learning
3
Step Three

Skill Building — Preparing for the World

As young adults, they need more than knowledge — they need capabilities. We offer structured skill training programmes in communication, digital literacy, trades, and leadership.

  • Vocational training programmes
  • Digital and communication skills
  • Confidence and leadership workshops
4
Step Four

Career Opportunities — The Beginning of a New Chapter

The final step is the most powerful: a first job, a first income, and the first moment of realising that the future they imagined is actually possible. We connect graduates with employers who believe in second chances.

  • Industry mentorship and placement support
  • Job-readiness and interview preparation
  • Employer partnerships and career fairs
